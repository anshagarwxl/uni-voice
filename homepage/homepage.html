WHAT IS UNIVOICE?

UNIvoice is a pioneering platform dedicated to empowering high school graduates by providing them with direct access to insights from college alumni and current students. We understand that choosing the right university is a crucial decision, and our mission is to ensure that you have all the information you need to make an informed choice. Read more

Our platform connects you with those who have firsthand experience of the colleges you're interested in. Whether it's understanding the campus culture, getting a sense of student life, or seeking advice on academic programs, UNIvoice offers a unique opportunity to hear directly from those who have been in your shoes.

By bridging the gap between prospective students and their potential future colleges, UNIvoice helps you navigate the complexities of university selection with confidence. Our goal is to make sure your voice is heard, and that you have access to the most accurate, up-to-date information possible, allowing you to embark on your academic journey with clarity and purpose.
